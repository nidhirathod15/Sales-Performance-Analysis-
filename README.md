# Sales-Performance-Analysis
## Overview

This project presents an executive-level Sales & Profit Performance dashboard built in Tableau to analyze overall revenue health, profitability trends, and regional performance.
The dashboard evaluates ₹2,297,201 in total sales and ₹286,397 in total profit, transforming transactional data into actionable business insights.

## Objective
To move beyond surface-level revenue tracking and answer:
* Are we growing profitably?
* Where is margin pressure emerging?
* Which regions drive performance?
* Which product categories create or destroy value?

## Key Metrics
* **Total Sales:** ₹2,297,201
* **Total Profit:** ₹286,397
* **Profit Ratio Range:** 7.9% – 14.9%
* **12.5% Month-over-Month Sales Decline Identified**
  
## Dashboard Components
### 1. KPI Summary

High-level visibility into total sales, profit, and profit ratio to assess overall business health.

### 2. Month-over-Month Trend Analysis
Dual-axis visualization comparing sales and profit trends.
Identified a 12.5% MoM decline signaling emerging margin pressure.

### 3. Regional Profitability Benchmarking
Compared regional contributions to profit.
* West: Highest-performing region
* Central: Underperforming region
Supports regional resource allocation decisions.

### 4. Sub-Category Performance Segmentation
Identified:
* High-margin drivers: Phones, Paper
* Loss-making segment: Tables
Enables product portfolio optimization and pricing strategy evaluation.

## Tools & Techniques
* Tableau
* Dual-axis visualization
* Profit ratio calculation
* Interactive filtering
* Trend & variance analysis
* Business KPI design

## Key Insights
* Revenue growth without margin stability creates risk.
* Regional disparities indicate reallocation opportunities.
* A small number of categories disproportionately influence profitability.
* Early detection of declining trends enables proactive intervention.
